Our Story

We started MYPIE in London in 2014, selling our pies from a 1970's Bedford van. Now you can buy them here, isn't that great?

At MYPIE, we don't just make pies. We lovingly create each flavour, each pie, one by one. It's no wonder we're one of the most awarded pie makers in the U.K.

Our weekly changing menu uses primarily British products, and we can track each one right back to the farm or brewery they come from. You don't often see that.

We love what we do and we're sure you will too.
